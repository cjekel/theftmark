Microdot Protection is an innovative security marking system for both identification and anti-theft applications. Each Microdot has unique information etched on the surface which can display; name, PIN, and telephone number or web-site. The Microdots are then mixed with a UV traceable adhesive that can be applied to almost any surface for permanent identification purposes. A Microdot kit has a container with over 1,000 Microdots, an applicator brush, and window warning labels. Microdots are simple to apply, and is recommended by Police and Insurance Companies.
